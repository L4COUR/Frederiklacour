about / cv

My name is Frederik la Cour, I am a sound designer and interactive audio specialist based in Copenhagen. I am interested in creating sounds and designing interactive audio systems that provides engaging and immersive audio experiences. So far I have created and implemented soundFX in video games, programmed and implemented sonification code on embedded hardware, taught audio programming using software and hardware tools and assisted in research and experimentation with spatial audio.

Designing adaptive systems that allows the listener to interact with sound in a meaningful way, that truely immerses them into the experience and evokes the desired emotions. Exploring new tools and approaches to sound design and applying them in practice in order to push the boundaries of what is possible with interactive audio is what drives my passion and curiousity for sound.

If you want me to create audio for your project, please don't hesitate to get in touch!
